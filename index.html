CLIMATE OF THE UKRAINIAN CARPATHIANS

The Carpathians are located in a moderate continental temperate zone.
The winters are mild with temperatures usually below freezing but not frigid.
Winter usually sets in in December and lasts till late March, with near continuous
snow cover for elevations over 800 m. Recently, winters have been warmer
and the snow has arrived later.

Spring in the Carpathians it is amazing time of nature waking up from
long winter dream. Snow melts, springs wake up and start chasing each other,
birds sing and spring sun warms!

And the summers are warm but not excessively hot.
Precipitation is abundant, but it is far from a rain forest. Summers see humid weather
and plenty of thunderstorms, during which one must stay off the peaks and ridges.
Biting flies can be a nuisance in areas.

Fall temperatures are cool. And the autumn colors
are brilliant in places, particularly west of the watershed where deciduous
trees are more prevalent.
